Help with Mathelo

Mathelo consists of a block-oriented layout within sessions. Each block coordinates in real time with other blocks on the other participants' screens in the sessions. To invite someone to your session, use the sidebar and select "Invite others."

The solution to the problem is devised in a dark gray box, by default in the lower right hand corner. To receive a printable version of the solution and the problem, select "Print solution" from the sidebar.

If you have any questions, contact the creators of Mathelo: Steven Schmatz and Amit Mizrahi.
