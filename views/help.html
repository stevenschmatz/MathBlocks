Help with Mathelo

Mathelo is a tool built for problem solvers.

We have given you tools to solve your everyday mathematical problems cooperatively.

Features

  • A minimalistic design that doesn't get in your way of productivity.
  • A menu-bar that you can move out of the way (with the white stripes) to give you a full workspace.
  • "Blocks" that you can move around and remove so you only have what you need.
  • Communication tools (video/text chat) for collaboration (you have to click "allow" for your camera to work with our app.
  • LaTeX (equation editing), graphing programs, and a numerical calculator for powerful problem-solving techniques.
  • Enter your final answer (with explanation, if possible) in the "solutions" column, and you can print it out on the side menu.
  • Note: You can enter LaTeX equations in several places with surrounding the code with dollar signs.
